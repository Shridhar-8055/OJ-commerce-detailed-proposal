Strategy Overview

Our approach prioritizes placements within already ranking, traffic-driven articles to deliver immediate referral value from Day 1. We combine niche edits (link insertions into existing content) with strategic new content placements for long-term brand authority.
2-3 / mo

Niche Edits / Link Insertions

Contextual link placements within existing articles that are already ranking and receiving organic traffic. This forms the majority of the 4 monthly backlinks.

  • Articles already indexed and ranking on Google
  • Proven organic traffic to the target page
  • Immediate referral traffic potential
  • Contextually relevant anchor text
  • In-content placement (no sidebar/footer)
1-2 / mo

New Content Placements

Guest contributions, PR features, and expert quotes on high-authority publications for brand visibility and long-term SEO equity.

  • Guest posts on sites with open contributor programs
  • HARO / expert quote placements (DR 80-95)
  • Product features and roundup inclusions
  • PR-driven editorial mentions
  • Builds brand authority over time

Why This Blended Approach?

Niche edits deliver immediate value — the article is already ranking, already getting traffic, and Google has already indexed it. Adding a contextual link to OJCommerce means referral traffic starts flowing immediately, without waiting months for a new article to rank.

New content placements build long-term authority — guest posts, expert quotes, and PR features create brand mentions across the web, diversify the backlink profile, and establish OJCommerce as a trusted name in the home/furniture space.

Together, they create a natural, sustainable link profile that Google rewards — a healthy mix of editorial mentions, contextual links, and branded references across high-authority domains.

What We Reject

We do not pursue placements on sites or pages that fail any of the following checks:

❌ Domain with no organic traffic or declining traffic trend
❌ Pages not indexed by Google or de-indexed
❌ Thin content pages (under 500 words)
❌ Pages with excessive outbound links (50+ external links)
❌ PBN (Private Blog Network) sites or link farms
❌ Sites with spam score above 5%
❌ Articles not updated in the past 18+ months
❌ Irrelevant niches (no topical connection to home/furniture)

Pre-Placement Validation Process

Before any placement is finalized, we run a 5-step validation:

1. Domain Audit — DR, traffic trend, spam score, backlink profile quality
2. Page-Level Check — Organic traffic, ranking keywords, keyword positions, index status
3. Content Review — Relevance, freshness, word count, editorial quality
4. Link Profile Check — Existing outbound links, anchor text distribution, nofollow ratio
5. Client Approval — Final placement URL shared with you for sign-off before the link goes live
